Tea without Milk!

So you dont have milk right? Don't worry! make an awesome tea while following these steps:

Black Tea:

Simply brew your black tea as usual, then add a dash of lemon juice or a slice of lemon for a citrusy twist.

Herbal Tea:

Many herbal teas are naturally caffeine-free and don't require milk. You can try chamomile, peppermint, or ginger tea for a soothing and flavorful beverage.

Earl Grey Tea:

Earl Grey tea is naturally fragrant and flavorful, so it doesn't need milk to taste great. You can brew it with a little bit of honey or lemon for added flavor.

Green Tea:

Green tea is another type of tea that can be enjoyed without milk. You can brew it with a little bit of honey or ginger for added flavor.
